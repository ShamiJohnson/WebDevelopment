Fat Loss

Although cardio is a great way to burn fat, there are other workout programs which will target fat loss not only during the workout but also outside of the gym. The most effective fat loss program includes High intensity interval training(HIIT)exercises. Some of these excercises are listed below. If you add these HIIT training in your cardio it will definetely help you shred all the extra body fat.

Some of these HIIT exercises are

  1. Jump rope finisher
  2. Sprint
  3. Lounges
  4. Box jumps
  5. Burpees
  6. Lateral Jumps
